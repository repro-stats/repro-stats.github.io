The ReproStats
ecosystem.

Four packages covering the full lifecycle of regulated R analysis — from audit and certification to estimand specification and data provenance.

reproducr On CRAN

The foundation. Audit, certify, and monitor R scripts and environments for reproducibility in regulated settings. Automates 21 CFR Part 11 compliance for your R analyses.

  • Script-level audit trail with cryptographic hash
  • Risk scoring for regulatory compliance
  • Environment certification and lockfiles
  • Drift detection across analysis runs
> install.packages("reproducr")
Docs & source →
regulog In development

Electronic audit logging for R, built to 21 CFR Part 11 standards. Provides structured, tamper-evident logging of all analytical actions with timestamps and user attribution.

  • Immutable audit log with digital signatures
  • User-attributed action tracking
  • Structured output for regulatory review
  • Integration with reproducr audit trail
> remotes::install_github("repro-stats/regulog")
View on GitHub →
lineager In development

Row-level data provenance for clinical datasets. Track every transformation, filter, and derivation applied to your data from raw input to analysis-ready ADaM datasets.

  • Row-level transformation tracking
  • SDTM to ADaM derivation audit trail
  • Visual provenance graph generation
  • CDISC-compatible lineage documentation
> remotes::install_github("repro-stats/lineager")
View on GitHub →
estimandr In development

ICH E9(R1) estimand framework for R. Define, document, and implement your estimand strategy — population, variable, intercurrent events, summary measure — directly in R.

  • Structured estimand specification objects
  • Intercurrent event strategy documentation
  • SAP-ready estimand output tables
  • Integration with common clinical trial packages
> remotes::install_github("repro-stats/estimandr")

About ReproStats

Why ReproStats
exists.

Pharmaceutical data science has a reproducibility problem. Most clinical analyses cannot be independently reproduced. Regulatory submissions fail because of undocumented environments and ad-hoc R scripts. Clinical decisions get made on results that were never designed to be verified.

ReproStats was built to fix this. We develop open-source R infrastructure that makes reproducibility and regulatory compliance the path of least resistance — not an afterthought bolted on at submission time.

Our packages are peer-reviewed, tested at 97% coverage, and designed from the ground up for GxP environments. Our consulting practice implements these tools in real clinical programmes — from Phase I to NDA submission.

ReproStats is based in Brussels, Belgium. We operate as ReproStats BV and work with pharma and biotech teams across Europe and globally. Our platform arm, Reprosia, brings AI to the reproducibility challenge at scale.
